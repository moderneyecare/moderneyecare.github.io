About our Doctor

Dr Biswajit Dey after completing his MBBS from Berhampur, Odisha, completed his post-graduation in Ophthalmology from Susrut Eye Foundation Kolkata which is the centre of Excellence for eye care in West Bengal and is famous for community eye care as well as advanced eye care in the state. Then he did his fellowship in Cataract, Pediatric Ophthalmology and Squint from Aravind Eye Hospital, which is considered to be the ‘Mecca’ of Community Eye care across the world. After spending 4 years there, he came back to Odisha with a vision to serve the people with the skill and compassion imbibed during my stay in Aravind Eye Hospital. Till date, he has performed more than 50,000 SICS, 10,000 Phaco, 2000 pediatric cataracts, 1000 squint surgeries apart from many other surgeries.

He has been a visiting consultant in Purnima Netralaya (Jamshedpur) , Rotary Eye Hospital (Balasore), JPM Rotary Eye Hospital (Cuttack), EKOS Rotary Eye Hopsital (Bhubaneswar), Ruby Eye Hospital (Berhampur) apart from many other private set ups. He has operated in many out-reach camps in Jharkhand and Odisha and have presented numerous research papers on Social and community eye care in National and International eye conferences and even won best paper award in those categories.
